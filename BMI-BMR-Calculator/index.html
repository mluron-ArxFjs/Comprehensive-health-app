BMR Calculator - Healthy Eating Tips







1. 1/2 of each of your meals should be composed of vegetables or fruits. Aim for a variety of colors.

2. Replace foods high in saturated fat with healthier options.

Ex: Replace whole milk with skim milk, or replace butter with olive oil.
3. Use natural sweeteners instead of added sugars. For instance, fruits and honey are better alternatives to multiple teaspoons of suagr.
4. Consume less than 2,300 mg of sodium per day. Check nutrition labels and protect your heart!
5. Choose water over sugary drinks! Water has 0 calories, regulates the body temperature, and cleanses the body of wastes.
